Delete Your suggestion food Account

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your suggestion food account and the data associated with it.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to chhayprogram@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
  2. Include the email address you used to sign in to the app (your Google account email).
  3. We will verify your identity and process your request within 7 business days.
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been deleted.
Quick request template — copy and send:

To: chhayprogram@gmail.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request

"I would like to delete my suggestion food account associated with the email address: [your Google sign-in email]"

What Gets Deleted

DataAction
Profile information (name, country, age, job type, diet restrictions, budget, spicy tolerance)Permanently deleted
Posts (photos, captions, dish names)Permanently deleted
Comments and likesPermanently deleted
Account authentication record (Firebase/Google Sign-In link)Permanently deleted

Retention

Once a deletion request is processed, your data is removed from our active database and storage (Supabase) within 7 business days. We do not retain backups containing your personal data beyond what is necessary for short-term disaster recovery, which are automatically purged on a rolling basis.

Questions?

Contact us at chhayprogram@gmail.com for any questions about this process or our Privacy Policy.
