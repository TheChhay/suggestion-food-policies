2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate your account — authenticate you via Google Sign-In and keep your profile and posts associated with your account.
  • To power the social feed — display your posts, photos, captions, likes, and comments to other users of the App.
  • To generate food suggestions — your profile attributes (country, diet restrictions, budget, spicy tolerance, etc.) are sent to a third-party AI provider (Anthropic's Claude API) to generate personalized food/dish suggestions. No content you post publicly (photos, captions) is sent for this purpose — only the profile fields needed to personalize suggestions.
  • To maintain and improve the App — diagnose bugs, understand feature usage, and improve reliability.

3. Third-Party Services We Use

We rely on the following service providers to operate the App. Each processes a subset of your data as described:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) — handles sign-in via your Google account. Firebase receives your basic Google account identity (name, email, profile photo) to authenticate you.
  • Supabase — our database and file storage provider. Your profile data, posts, comments, likes, and uploaded photos are stored on Supabase's infrastructure.
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — processes selected profile fields (country, diet restrictions, budget, spicy tolerance, age range) to generate personalized food suggestions. Anthropic does not receive your name, email, photos, or posts.

Each of these providers maintains its own privacy and security practices, independent of this App.

4. Public Content

This App is a social platform. Photos, captions, dish names, and comments you post are visible to other users of the App by design. Please do not post anything you wish to keep private.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Supabase (database and file storage) and Firebase (authentication). We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data and posts for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, your profile data and posts will be removed from our active systems within a reasonable period, except where retention is required for legal or security purposes.
